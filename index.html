Case Study 2

IRCTC Landing Page Redesign

"A total visual overhaul of India's official Railway Portal"

Every time I open Government service portals I've noticed something. The Government didn't cared about the User Interface and User Experience. Yes the IRCTC portal was fast and error free considering the amount of people uses it daily. The initial thought I had was Government websites are for providing service and they doesn't need the fancy minimalist design.

Until I explored developed country's social service pages and found out that they are up-to date with the design while India is still stuck in 2000s. So the problem is not usability. It’s just being lazy.
